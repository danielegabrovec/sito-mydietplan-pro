We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the service and while the account is active. Upon account deletion, personal and health data are erased (see §7). Tax/payment data is retained for the period required by law (typically 10 years).

6. Where data is stored and local data

Synced data is stored in the cloud database (Supabase) protected by per-user security rules (Row Level Security). The app also keeps a functional copy in your device's localStorage; this copy is cleared on logout and on account deletion to protect your privacy on shared devices. localStorage is used strictly for technical/functional purposes (not for third-party advertising profiling).

To exercise your rights or lodge a complaint, write to info.dottdanielegabrovec@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (garanteprivacy.it) or your local supervisory authority.
