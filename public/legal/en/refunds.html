1. Seller and invoicing

Purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy, acting as Merchant of Record: it is the official seller, issues the invoice and handles VAT and payments. These terms apply together with the Lemon Squeezy buyer terms.

2. Free trial

Subscription plans include a 7-day free trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you are not charged. If you do not cancel, the chosen plan is billed at the end of the trial.

3. Right of withdrawal (EU consumers)

Under EU consumer law you have the right to withdraw within 14 days of purchase.

Digital content/services — important. By activating immediate access to the digital service, you are asked to consent to immediate performance and to acknowledge that, once use has begun, the right of withdrawal may be lost for the part already used. The free trial lets you evaluate the service at no cost.

4. Refunds

  • Subscriptions (Monthly/Annual): you can cancel anytime; cancellation stops future renewals and access remains active until the end of the period already paid. Amounts already invoiced for the current period are generally non-refundable, unless otherwise required by law.
  • Lifetime (one-time): refund requests are assessed within 14 days of purchase, barring abuse.
  • Approved refunds are issued by Lemon Squeezy to the original payment method.

5. How to cancel your subscription

  • In the app: Profile → Manage subscription → Manage on Lemon Squeezy (customer billing portal).
  • Alternatively, via the "Manage subscription" link in Lemon Squeezy emails.
  • Deleting your account in the app also attempts to automatically cancel the recurring subscription to avoid future charges.
